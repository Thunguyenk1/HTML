E-Mail Editor Help.

  • To and Cc:
    To and CC boxes allow you to enter the e-mail address of the recepoents to whom to message has to be sent. Recipients will be able to see who else was sent the message by looking at the contents of the To and Cc boxes.
  • Subject:
    The Subject box allows you to enter meaningful summary of the message.
  • Message:
    The Message TextArea allows you to enter the text of your message.
